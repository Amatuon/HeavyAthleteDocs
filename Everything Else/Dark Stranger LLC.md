## The Name's Origin
The company name has an interesting backstory rooted in Gaelic:
- McDougal is an Americanized version of MacDougall
- Mac = son of
- Dou = dark, secret, or mysterious
- Gall = stranger
- So, McDougal essentially means "son of a dark stranger"
